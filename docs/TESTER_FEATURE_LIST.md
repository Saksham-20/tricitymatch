# TricityShadi — Try It Out 💍

A matrimonial site for the Tricity (Chandigarh · Mohali · Panchkula).
Thanks for helping test it! Here's what to try. Takes ~10 minutes.

**Website:** https://tricityshadi.com

---

## 1. Create your profile
- Sign up with your **email or mobile number**.
- You'll get a **one-time code (OTP)** to confirm it — enter it to continue.
- Fill in the basics (name, age, city). You can complete the rest later.

> Tip: use real-ish details so matching feels realistic. It's a test site — no need to share anything you're not comfortable with.

## 2. Browse & search
- Go to **Find Match** to see other profiles.
- Try the **filters** (age, city, education, etc.) and the **sort** options.
- Open any profile to see full details, **compatibility %**, and **horoscope match**.

## 3. Show interest
- **Express Interest** (heart) on someone you like.
- **Shortlist** (bookmark) profiles to save for later.

## 4. Your matches
- Open the **Matches** page — three tabs:
  - **Saved** — profiles you bookmarked
  - **Mutual** — people who liked you back
  - **Likes You** — people who've shown interest

## 5. Chat
- When you and someone **both** show interest, you can **message** each other.
- Open **Messages**, pick a conversation, and send a chat.

## 6. Get verified (optional)
- Go to **Verification** and take a **live selfie**.
- Our team checks it against your photos to give you a **verified badge**.
- The selfie is never shown to other members.

## 7. Extra things to poke at
- **Settings → Privacy** — control who can see your profile.
- **Profile → Edit** — add photos, bio, preferences, horoscope details.
- **Subscription** — have a look at the plans (no need to actually pay).
- **Guardian / Family** — you can set up a profile for a family member.
- **Report / Block** — try it on a profile to see how safety tools work.
